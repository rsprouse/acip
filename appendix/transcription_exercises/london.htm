London accent

Once there was a young rat named Arthur,

who could never make up his mind.

Whenever his friends asked him if he would like to go out with them,

He would  only answer, "I don't know."

He wouldn't say "yes" or "no" either.

He would always shirk making a choice.

 

I am training to be a courier in Central London

I want a full time post with a company called Camelot

the course lasting four to ten weeks

I live in North London and it takes me an hour to ride in, and that is before I even start for the day

I need to know where everything is in London

including streets, squares, parks , postcodes,

landmarks and all sorts

My course will eventually lead to a guaranteed full time job

where I will hopefully progress onto a motorcycle.
