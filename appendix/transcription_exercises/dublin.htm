Dublin accent

Once there was a young rat named Arthur,

who could never make up his mind.

Whenever his friends asked him

if he would like to go out with them,

H would only answer, "I don't know." He wouldn't say "yes" or "no" either.

He would always shirk making a choice.

 

I'm living in an apartment on Bachelor's walk in the centre of Dublin.

Bachelor's Walk is on the banks of the River Liffey.

It's very central, and the apartment's really nice.

It's a one bedroom apartment looking onto a lovely courtyard.

It's on the top floor, and from the balcony I can see the spike on O'Connel St.

It's nice and quiet, even though it's in the very heart of the city.
