Magnetic Resonance Imaging of the vocal tract:

University of Oxford

This clip is a real-time MRI sequence of the utterance "answered a door". One difficulty with MRI imaging of the vocal tract is that the machine is very noisy. This movie has been processed to remove the MRI noise and consequently sounds high-pass filtered.

Many thanks to John Coleman, director of the Oxford Phonetics lab for providing this video.
