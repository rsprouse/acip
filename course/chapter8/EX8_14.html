Wideband and narrowband spectrograms of Is Pat sad, or mad?

This version of Is Pat mad, or sad? is shown with a pitch trace, instead of the narrowband spectrogram. Notice how the pitch pulses in the spectrogram are closer together when the pitch is high. This is especially noticeable at time 1.7 seconds.
