Chapter 10 - Performance Exercise E

See if you can say these utterances backwards. It is best to first record yourself and make a careful narrow phonetic transcription - then reverse the transcription and try to read it.

One way to check your progress is to play your version backwards (in WaveSurfer or Praat, etc.).
